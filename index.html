Thames Youth Orchestra is really good, write some more guff about the orchestra, and what we offer our players.

Thames Youth Orchestra was founded in 2005 and is based in South West London.

TYO now has a permanent staff of professional musicians and comprises more than sixty young players drawn from numerous local schools. Its ethos is defined by a challenging, adventurous approach to programming, which combines well-established large-scale orchestral favourites with twentieth century rarities and new commissions.

TYO has featured in a BBC programmes, performed regularly at London's Cadogan Hall and played to full houses in venues across Europe.

A sister orchestra, the Thames Youth Sinfonia has been launched for players at Grades 4 and above.
