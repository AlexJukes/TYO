Thames Youth Orchestra is one of the leading youth orchestras in London

Thames Youth Orchestra is a full symphony orchestra comprising more than sixty young musicians drawn from over thirty South West London schools and beyond.

Founded in 2005, TYO has gone from strength to strength, performing at prestigious London venues including the Royal Albert Hall, St. John’s Smith Square, LSO St Luke’s, Cadogan Hall and the Barbican Centre. TYO also undertakes annual overseas tours, and has played to packed audiences at major venues across Europe.

TYO now has a permanent staff of professional musicians and comprises more than sixty young players drawn from numerous local schools. Its ethos is defined by a challenging, adventurous approach to programming, which combines well-established large-scale orchestral favourites with twentieth century rarities and new commissions.

TYO also has a sister ensemble, the Thames Youth Jazz Orchestra, for talented young big band musicians.
